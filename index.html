GITILGARH

A beautiful village

ABOUT

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Gitilgarh village is 363053. Gitilgarh village is located in Kairo subdivision of Lohardaga district in Jharkhand, India. It is situated 10km away from sub-district headquarter Kairo (tehsildar office) and 38km away from district headquarter Lohardaga. As per 2009 stats, Hanhat is the gram panchayat of Gitilgarh village.



The total geographical area of village is 494 hectares. Gitilgarh has a total population of 1,538 peoples, out of which male population is 759 while female population is 779. Literacy rate of gitilgarh village is 50.91% out of which 59.42% males and 42.62% females are literate. There are about 289 houses in gitilgarh village. Pincode of gitilgarh village locality is 835302.



NOTE: Kairo is nearest town to gitilgarh village for all major economic activities.
